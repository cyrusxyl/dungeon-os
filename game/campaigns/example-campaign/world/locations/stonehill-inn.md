# Stonehill Inn

The Stonehill Inn stands in the eastern part of Phandalin, a modest two-story building with a freshly painted sign showing a stone hill at sunset. The scent of fresh bread and roasting meat wafts from within, and you can hear the low murmur of conversation and occasional laughter.

## Atmosphere
Warm and welcoming, with a crackling fireplace casting dancing shadows on the wooden walls. The common room has several sturdy tables and a well-stocked bar.

## NPCs Present
- Toblen Stonehill (innkeeper)
- Trilena Stonehill (Toblen's wife, helps with the inn)
- Pip and Gwyn (Toblen's young children, playing near the fireplace)

## Services
- Room: 5 sp per night
- Meals: 1 sp (simple), 3 sp (hearty)
- Ale: 4 cp per mug
- Wine: 1 sp per glass

## Exits
- Front door: Leads to town square
- Back door: Leads to kitchen and storage area
- Stairs: Lead to guest rooms on second floor

## Notable Features
- Large stone fireplace, always well-tended
- Local bulletin board with notices and rumors
- Cozy guest rooms upstairs (6 available)
